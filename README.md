# Factors-Affecting-Intension-to-Purchase-a-Residential-Property

**Project Details:**

  * **Title:** Factors Affecting Intension to Purchase a Residential Property
  * **Company Mentor:** Bharat Panwar (Sr. Consultant), JLL
  * **Submitted By:** SHUBHAM SRIVASTWA (21423MBA047)
  * **Research Design:** Exploratory Research
  * **Population:** People Living in Varanasi and Patna
  * **Sample Size:** 100
  * **Key Findings:**
      * Variables can be categorized into four factors: Construction location and quality factor, supply factor, finance factor, and superstition factor.
      * The most important variables are the location of the property, proximity to amenities, and power supply.
      * Maintenance costs are the least important variables considered by home buyers.
      * Construction location and quality factor has the most impact on purchase intention, with a significant relation also found for the supply factor.
      * The majority of respondents want their residential property in the mid-town (38.0%), followed by the outskirts of cities (27.0%), or a posh area of town (25.0%).
